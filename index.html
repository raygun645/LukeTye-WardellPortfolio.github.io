Introduction

I am Luke (Tye) Tye-Wardell, a driven and committed game developer and programmer currently specializing in AI and gameplay systems. As a graduate and current master's student at Staffordshire University, I hold a bachelor's degree in games development and am currently pursuing a master's degree in games programming. C++, Unreal Engine, and Unity are just a few of the programming languages and development environments I have refined my abilities and gained considerable experience with during my time as a student.

I aspire to one day become an AI engineer in game development, designing intricate and intelligent systems that adapt to players and create truly amazing gameplay experiences for everyone. I love trying to solve complex programming challenges and work to continuously refine my skills as a games programmer

I encourage you to browse through my portfolio, which includes a selection of my work that demonstrates my technical skills, inventiveness, and passion for the game industry. I'm thrilled to connect with you and share my passion for game creation, whether you're here to view my projects, read more about my background, or talk about possible prospects.

Thank you for visiting, I hope you enjoy my work as much as I enjoyed developing it.

Skills

Here are the skills that i feel best define my personal skillset as a games developer

Artifical inteligence in games

  • Adaptive AI systems using state machines and behavior trees
  • NPC behavior logic and dynamic difficulty balancing
  • Experimenting with core AI concepts and custom systems

Procedural Content Generation

  • Procedural dungeon generation using Cellular Automata, Binary Space Partitioning and Depth-First Search
  • Procedural asset placement with Poisson Disk Sampling, Wave-Function collapse and hybrid algorithms
  • Procedural asset generation with in-game Weapons, enemies and Items

Gameplay Systems

  • Designing and implementing modular gameplay mechanics
  • Working with reactive and dynamic systems to enhance player immersion
  • Recreating gameplay systems from popular games

Programming Skils

  • Unreal Engine 5 with a Strong focus on C++ gameplay development
  • Experience in Unity with C# scripting and rapid prototyping
  • Comfortable with IDEs like Rider, Visual Studio, and debugging/version control tools
